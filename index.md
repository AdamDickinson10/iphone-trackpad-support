# iPhone Trackpad for Mac Support

iPhone Trackpad for Mac lets your iPhone work as a wireless trackpad for your Mac using your local Wi-Fi network.

## Requirements

* iPhone Trackpad for Mac installed on your iPhone
* Trackpad Receiver for Mac installed on your Mac
* Both devices connected to the same local Wi-Fi network
* Accessibility permission enabled for Trackpad Receiver on your Mac

## Getting Started

1. Open Trackpad Receiver for Mac on your Mac.
2. Open iPhone Trackpad for Mac on your iPhone.
3. Make sure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network.
4. When the app shows “Connected,” use your iPhone screen as a trackpad.

## Troubleshooting

If the app does not connect:

* Make sure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network.
* Quit and reopen both apps.
* Check that Trackpad Receiver has Accessibility permission on your Mac.
* Restart your Mac if macOS permissions seem stuck.
